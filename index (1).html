The Mission

During the April 2026 Tamil Nadu Legislative Assembly Election, police officers across Vellore District needed a fast, reliable way to locate poll booths, get live GPS-based turn-by-turn directions, check distances, travel times, and identify nearby landmarks — without any login or training required.

This tool was deployed at vellore-police.zephor.org and served officers across multiple police station jurisdictions. It also provided an emergency SMS alert system to notify senior officers of EV machine issues or law-and-order incidents at any booth. The election cycle has concluded. The mission was a success.

What It Did

  • Live GPS-based turn-by-turn directions to any poll booth
  • Filter booths by police station jurisdiction
  • Distance & estimated travel time display
  • Landmark information for every booth
  • Emergency alert via SMS to senior officers
  • Admin portal for booth data management
  • Bulk Excel import for booth records
  • Mobile-first, no-login public interface
